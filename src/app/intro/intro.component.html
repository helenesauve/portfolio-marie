Marie Sauvé

Multimedia Journalist

I’m a multimedia journalist with extensive professional experience across radio, documentary filmmaking, and disinformation investigation. Currently based in Dublin, with strong roots in the UK, where I lived for nearly a decade, I work as a verification journalist at Storyful, covering global news events ranging from conflicts in the Middle East and U.S. politics to European affairs, extreme weather events, and, occasionally, bear season.

Prior to specialising in verification, I worked with national broadcasters such as the BBC and RTÉ, and contributed to international documentary productions. Beyond journalism, I’m an avid consumer of the arts—regularly attending galleries, the theatre, opera, and cinema (perhaps more than I should admit). I also write film reviews, essays, and fiction.
