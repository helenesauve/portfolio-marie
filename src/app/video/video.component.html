Video

Filming has always been a medium close to my heart, and I have been working with a camera for over 10 years, both professionally and in my personal time.

I edited this 'Untold Stories' video designed to capture the voices of the people of Derry, narrowing the broad notion of borders to more intimate and perhaps underserved ones.

For this video, I produced, directed and edited a video clip for Derry singer-songwriter Noella Hutton.
