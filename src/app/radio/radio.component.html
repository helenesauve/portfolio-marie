Radio

As a music broadcaster, I produced and hosted a weekly three-hour music show for a the community radio station Drive 105. From January 2020 until June 2022, I curated and produced a 90mn-long specialist programme.

As a journalist with BBC Northern Ireland, I produced news and current affairs programmes such as the Breakfast Show and The Mark Patterson Show, writing broadcast scripts ready for transmission. I also researched stories and interviewed talents to produce audio packages that I edited for programmes of various genres.”

Commissioned by RTÉ, I had the opportunity to research stories and record audio packages and vox pops on issues occurring in Northern Ireland.
